Welcome To Apprentice Brewing

Who We Are

Apprentice Brewing was founded to educate and engage people in the creative world of homebrewing. Every brewer has their first batch, and we're here to help you make that first step. From the beer enthusiast to the sporadic drinker, there's something here for everyone. There's something here for everyone.

Brewing your own beer can be highly rewarding and can open up many opportunities to connect with people. Get your friends over for a tasting party, brew as a shared project or for your personal enjoyment, or simply as a cheap alternative to mainstream beers. And if you're still not convinced, a 40-pint batch can cost you as little as 40p per pint!

Learn how to brew your first batch with our step-by-step guide, and browse our recommended retailers, beer kit and tutorial videos for tips on how to get started. If you're interested in finding out more, get in touch with us by sending a message below. So get learning, brewing, tasting your very own liquid courage!
